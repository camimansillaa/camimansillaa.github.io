Turning simple earth into radiant beauty, a mindful craft of patience, polish, and harmony. Learn the art, explore techniques, and share your creations.

What is Dorodango?

A traditional Japanese pastime where mud is molded, dried, and polished until it gleams like glass. It’s a quiet blend of art and meditation.

Why It Matters

Beyond aesthetics, dorodango teaches mindfulness, patience, and appreciation for natural materials — values deeply rooted in Japanese culture.
