The Dorodango Community

Around the world, artists, students, and hobbyists connect through the peaceful craft of Hikaru Dorodango. Join our circle to learn, share, and celebrate this meditative art.

Our Shared Values

  • Creativity — turning soil into something beautiful.
  • Mindfulness — finding calm in each rotation.
  • Respect for nature — every dorodango starts from the earth.

Community Activities

  1. Workshops and tutorials:
    • Monthly online sessions with artists from Japan and abroad.
    • Hands-on guidance for beginners.
  2. Gallery Showcases:
    • Virtual exhibitions hosted quarterly.
    • Open calls for international submissions.
