How to Make a Hikaru Dorodango

Watch the Process

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Gather Materials
    • Fine soil or clay-rich earth
    • Small sieve or strainer
    • Water and a clean cloth
  2. Form the Core — Mix a little water into soil until it packs, then roll it into a sphere about the size of an orange.
  3. Dry Slowly — Let the ball rest and re-roll gently as it dries to avoid cracks.
  4. Layer and Polish — Add finer dry soil layers, polishing between each until smooth and glossy.
  5. Final Shine — Buff the surface with a cloth or flour dust until it reflects light.
