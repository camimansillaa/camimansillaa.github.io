Bloom & Root Flower Shop Opens

Locally-Sourced Flowers and Workshops

A brand-new flower shop called Bloom & Root has officially opened its doors on Main Street, offering fresh, locally-sourced blooms and a warm, welcoming atmosphere. The shop is owned by longtime gardener and floral designer Mia Chen, who says her goal is to bring “a little joy and beauty to people's everyday lives.”

With its cozy decor, calming scents, and hand-tied bouquets, Bloom & Root has already drawn in curious passersby and flower lovers alike. The shop offers everything from classic roses and sunflowers to more unique seasonal arrangements. It also hosts DIY bouquet workshops every Saturday.

"This place is magical,” said customer Olivia Harper, who attended the grand opening. “I’ve never felt so relaxed in a store before.”

In a statement to local press, Chen explained her passion for the project: Flowers are more than decorations—they’re emotions, memories, and stories.

Bloom & Root is open seven days a week and has plans to offer delivery services starting next month!
